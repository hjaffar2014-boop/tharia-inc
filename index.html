Ali Tharia is the Founder and Principal Consultant at Tharia Inc., a boutique management consulting practice based in Toronto, Ontario. His mandate is clear: help businesses expand globally, transition leadership across generations, and transform exponentially.

Ali is a change maker who adapts his leadership style to the precise needs of each organisation. When strategy is the priority, he operates at the board level. When execution demands it, he becomes operationally hands-on — the rare advisor who can shift registers without losing effectiveness.

His eighteen-year career with the Toyota Group shaped a practitioner of unusual depth. He progressed from launching a greenfield manufacturing plant to Assistant Vice President at Toyota Tsusho Canada — along the way leading four distinct Canadian start-ups across Supply Chain, International Trade, Urban Mining, and Tier 1 Auto Parts Manufacturing.

Through extensive international work, Ali developed and implemented global growth initiatives across Japan, Taiwan, China, the UAE, Qatar, and Germany. He currently serves on the Entrepreneurship Advisory Council at the Ted Rogers School of Management, Toronto Metropolitan University.
